about me

who I am

My name is Emma, and I'm currently in my third year at Harvard University studying computer science with a minor in studio art. I'm from north Jersey but reside in Cambridge, Massachusetts, which has not been too different to be honest.

what I like

I care a lot about making things work well and look good. I like design that is both elegant and whimsical, and I have fun challenging myself to adapt to different styles.
