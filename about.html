about me

who I am

My name is Emma, and I'm currently in my third year at Harvard University studying computer science with a minor in studio art. I work on the design team of the harvard advocate, our very old and very loved literary magazine, where I design issue covers and layout. I also am a dj on the record hospital, the underground/punk department of WHRB. Besides DJing, I design posters and other promotional materials for the record hospital's concerts and radio events. Evidence of this can be found under the graphic design section of this site.

what I like

I care a lot about making things work well and look good. I like design that is both elegant and whimsical, and I have fun challenging myself to adapt to different styles.
