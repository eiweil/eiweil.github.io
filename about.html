hello

I work at the intersection of tech, policy, and equity. for me, this means helping people understand the disparate impact of specific technologies on vulnerable communities and individuals, in order to create better policy. my general philosphy is that unchecked efficiency often furthers the goals of those in power and exacerbates existing inequalities, that transparency without opportunities for shifts in power often works only to convince the public of expert opinion, and that technological responses alone cannot solve social or political problems.

when I decide to not be extremely un-fun, I like punk music and biking and listening to punk music while biking to buy more vegetables to make the same 5 vegan dishes I know how to make.
